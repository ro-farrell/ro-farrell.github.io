My name is Ryan O'Farrell

With an apostrophe

Not "OFarrell" or "Ofarrell". It's amazing how many programmers don't know that last names can have apostrophes in them.

An entirely separate source of headaches comes from the fact that there are multiple characters that look like apostrophes. Most platforms default to U+0027 APOSTROPHE ('), but Apple Keyboard in its infinite wisdom defaults to U+2019 RIGHT SINGLE QUOTATION MARK (’). Programmers do not seem to realize this.
